This is boinq

boinq is a platform under development for bio-ontology integrated querying of sequence annotations (or features), developed in a collaboration between the University of Ghent (the twist and biobix departments) and the company Genohm.

boinq uses FALDO and the Sequence Ontology for annotating sequence features and connects with bioportal for browsing external ontology terms.
